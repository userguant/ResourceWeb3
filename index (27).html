About the Archive

The Sanrio Character Archive is a resource dedicated to documenting the emotional universe of Sanrio's characters. Each character carries its own mood, personality, and way of being in the world.

This project began as a way to explore how character design communicates feeling — how a round shape, a soft colour, or a specific accessory can evoke comfort, joy, or nostalgia.

The archive is organized by mood, type, and era. Browse to discover characters that resonate with you, and save the ones that feel meaningful.

During testing, feedback revealed that users didn't just want to browse — they wanted to interact, collect, and build something personal. This insight led to a significant shift: moving from a static archive toward an interactive emotional experience where characters become the building blocks of a digital gift. That evolution became Memory Gift Atelier.
