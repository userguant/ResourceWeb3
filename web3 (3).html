About the Archive

The Sanrio Character Archive is a resource dedicated to documenting the rich emotional universe of Sanrio's characters. Each character carries its own mood, personality, and way of being in the world.

This project began as a way to explore how character design communicates feeling — how a round shape, a soft color, or a specific accessory can evoke comfort, joy, or nostalgia.

During testing, something became clear: users didn't just want to browse characters. They wanted to interact with them — to collect the ones that resonated, to build something personal. The "Save" feature was the first sign of this shift.

This archive is the draft phase of the project. The final version — Memory Gift Atelier — evolved directly from what was learned here: that characters aren't just reference entries. They're emotional building blocks for a gift.
