About This Archive

The Sanrio Character Archive is a resource dedicated to documenting and celebrating the rich history of Sanrio's character universe. Each character carries its own emotional personality — a mood, a world, a way of being.

This archive began as a way to explore how character design communicates feeling. Browsing by mood, type, or era, users can discover characters that resonate with them personally.

During testing, feedback revealed that users didn't just want to browse characters — they wanted to interact with them, collect them, and build something personal. This insight led to a significant shift in the project's direction: moving from a static archive toward an interactive emotional experience, where characters become the building blocks of a digital gift.

This draft represents the archive phase of the project. The final version — Memory Gift Atelier — evolved directly from what was learned here.
